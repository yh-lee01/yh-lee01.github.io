Proposed Spectral Contrastive Flow Matching (SCFM), a Flow Matching-based generative framework for audio-visual sound separation that introduces contrastive objectives to improve spectral discrimination across concurrent sound sources. Also designed Energy-Guided Calibration (EGC) to address energy imbalance between separated sources.
