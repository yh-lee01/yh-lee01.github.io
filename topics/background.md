> *"What I cannot create, I do not understand."*  
> — Richard Feynman

While writing my first paper and preparing for a Ph.D. program, I realized that impactful research requires more than just understanding the latest algorithms—it demands deep mathematical intuition and the ability to adapt foundational knowledge. In the rapidly evolving field of AI, specific methods may become obsolete, but the core principles remain constant.

To transition from simply solving problems to actively defining them, I am using this series as a final, comprehensive review to revisit and solidify these fundamental concepts. This is my effort to firmly anchor my understanding before taking the next step in my research journey.
